# Diabetes RAG Screening Assistant

A Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system using Indonesian diabetes guidelines.

Disclaimer:
This RAG assistant only screens the risk of pre-diabetes and type 2 diabetes, we highly advise you to not 
use this assistant outside of its intended purpose.

⚠️ Disclaimer  
This Risk Assessment Tool is intended solely for research and learning purposes.  
The results are **not clinically validated** and must not be interpreted as medical advice,  
diagnosis, or treatment. Patients should consult licensed healthcare professionals  
for accurate medical evaluation and decision-making.

## Features
- Ask diabetes questions with grounded references
- Rule-based risk screening
